Grand Theft Auto

The revolutionary gaming saga that would change gaming forever

From 1996 up to this day, the Grand Theft Auto (GTA) saga has made its name to even non-gaming people. Here's a timeline covering up some of the saga's most important dates:

  • 1996: The GTA series was born.
  • 1997: The first GTA was launched for PC, where it would be the baseline for the upcoming GTA titles
  • June 1999: GTA London was launched, an expansion pack for the first GTA in PC and PlayStation, setting the game in 1969 London
  • October 1999: GTA II was launched for PC and other consoles, where it had some new features compared to its predecesor
  • October 2001: GTA III was launched, where one of its key features was a third person view and a 3D world based in New York City called Liberty City. The game also had a storyline around the main character.
  • October 2002: GTA Vice City was launched, where it had a lot of the esence of its predecesor but in a whole new city based on 1986 Miami called Vice City. The game also had a different story, with a strong inspiration in the movie Scarface.
  • October 2004: GTA San Andreas was launched. It's inspired by the 1992's gangster wars that ocurred in various cities accross USA. The game is based in the fictious state of San Andreas, where players could play between three cities: Los Santos, based upon Los Angeles; San Fierro, based upon San Francisco; and Las Venturas, based upon Las Vegas.
  • 2005 - 2006: GTA Liberty City Stories and GTA Vice City Stories are launched, where the new story is based 3 and 2 years before the main story of its predecesors, respectively.
  • April 2008: GTA IV was launched. With it came the biggest graphical improvements over its predecesors with new HD graphics, taking advantage of the newer consoles that were launched around this period. The game is based in a refreshed version of Liberty City.
  • 2009: GTA IV: The Lost and Damned and GTA IV: The Ballad of Gay Tony are launched as expansion packs of GTA IV, with new stories that take place between the main game's story.
  • 2011: GTA V is announced, and with it came huge hype in the gaming community as this was going to be one the most ambitious games ever.
  • 2013: GTA V is launched, beside its online version, GTA: online. The game is based in a refreshed version of the city of Los Santos, along with some countryside that was available in GTA: San Andreas and also some new areas to explore.

The GTA saga has also come along with many controversies because of its violent and sexual content. But, in reality, lets face it, this has only boosted up the saga's popularity in the past 26 years it has existed. Eventhought gamers know what to expect with GTA games, this "free" advertisement is what makes it's way into non-gaming people as those are the ones that actually feel ofended by the game's content.
